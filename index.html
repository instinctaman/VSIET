About VSIET

The Vikram Sarabhai Institute of Engineering & Technology (VSIET) is more than just a college—it is a transformative educational hub known for innovation, excellence, and impact. VSIET prepares students for future careers by equipping them with cutting-edge knowledge, relevant skills, and a growth-oriented mindset in the dynamic fields of engineering and management. VSIET is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Ministry of Education, Government of India, and is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. The institute offers academically rigorous programs infused with practical applications, ensuring students master both theoretical foundations and hands-on experience tailored to industry needs. What sets VSIET apart is its mission to nurture not just employable graduates, but socially responsible leaders ready to make meaningful contributions. Our students are encouraged to innovate, conduct impactful research, and contribute to community development through academic, entrepreneurial, and service initiatives.
